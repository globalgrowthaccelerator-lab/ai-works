Process

The Production Pipeline

A rigorous, stage-by-stage process that ensures every audiobook meets broadcast quality standards.

1

Manuscript Intake & Preparation

We receive your manuscript and run it through our preparation pipeline — cleaning formatting, flagging pronunciation edge cases, and structuring the text for voice synthesis. We handle all common formats: DOCX, PDF, ePub, and plain text.

2

Voice Selection & Configuration

We work with you to select the right AI voice — from a library of hundreds of natural-sounding voices across accents, genders, and ages. For authors who want it, we also offer AI voice cloning from a short sample of your own voice.

3

AI Narration & Multi-Voice Casting

The manuscript is narrated using our AI synthesis pipeline. For fiction with multiple characters, we cast distinct voices for each character and handle dialogue attribution automatically. For non-fiction, we apply pacing and emphasis tuned to the genre.

4

Audio Processing & Mastering

Raw audio goes through noise reduction, normalization, EQ, and mastering — all tuned to ACX and Audible specifications. We also handle chapter splitting, silence padding, and metadata tagging.

5

Quality Review

Every audiobook goes through human quality review before delivery. Our reviewers check for mispronunciations, pacing issues, audio artifacts, and formatting compliance. We iterate until the audio is right.

6

Delivery & Publishing Support

We deliver the finished files and provide full publishing support — including guidance on ACX, Findaway Voices, and direct distribution. We can also handle submission on your behalf.

Quality Model

AI Speed. Human Ears.

We don't believe in fully automated audiobook production. AI handles the heavy lifting — synthesis, processing, file management — at speeds no human team can match. But every output is reviewed by a human before it reaches you.

Our quality reviewers listen for mispronunciations, unnatural pacing, audio artifacts, and anything that would break the listener's experience. We iterate until the audio is right — not until the clock runs out.

This hybrid model gives you the economics of AI with the standards of professional audio production.

AI-First Production Synthesis, processing, and file management handled by AI — dramatically reducing cost and turnaround time.
Human Quality Review Every chapter reviewed by a trained listener before delivery. We don't ship audio we wouldn't be proud to hear ourselves.
Iterate Until Right Revisions are part of the process. You'll see proofs before final delivery and have the opportunity to request changes.
