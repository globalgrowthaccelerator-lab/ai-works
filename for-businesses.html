What We've Built for Businesses

Real-world AI systems across industries — each one addressing a specific operational bottleneck.

Automated Lead Research Pipeline

A B2B SaaS company was spending 3 hours per SDR per day researching prospects. We built an AI pipeline that monitors LinkedIn, company websites, and news sources — automatically identifying qualified leads, enriching contact data, writing personalized outreach notes, and delivering a daily list directly to their CRM. Time per lead: reduced from 15 minutes to under 60 seconds.

Invoice & Contract Extraction

A financial services firm was manually extracting data from hundreds of invoices and contracts each month. We built a document processing pipeline that reads any document format, extracts key fields, validates against business rules, and exports structured data to their accounting system — with a confidence score and human review queue for edge cases.

Internal Knowledge Assistant

A professional services firm with thousands of documents, SOPs, and archived reports had no fast way to find information. We built a RAG-powered internal assistant that searches across all their knowledge — answering questions with cited sources, usable in Slack and their existing internal tools. Onboarding time for new staff dropped by 40%.

Operational Reporting Automation

A logistics company was spending two days each week manually compiling operational reports from multiple systems. We built an AI pipeline that collects data from all sources, identifies anomalies, writes narrative summaries, and delivers formatted reports on schedule — with alerts for anything requiring human attention.
