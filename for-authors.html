How We Support Authors & Publishers

Whether you have one book or a hundred, we have a production path built for you.

For Independent Authors

You've written the book. Now get it to listeners. We handle everything from voice selection and narration through to final delivery-ready files — no studio bookings, no narrators to brief, no audio editing software required. Just share your manuscript and we take it from there.

For Publishing Houses

Scale your catalogue with a production partner who can handle volume. We build dedicated workflows for publishers — consistent quality across titles, fast turnaround, and the option for custom author portals so your authors can track their own productions.

Multi-Language Production

Reach global audiences by producing your audiobook in multiple languages. We translate the manuscript and produce in the target language with native-accent AI voices — maintaining the original tone and intent of the text.

Voice Cloning

Want the audiobook to sound like you? We offer AI voice cloning from a short recording sample — letting you narrate your own book without sitting in a studio for days. Your voice, your book, your listeners' experience.

Why Audiobooks

Unlock New Value From Your Existing Work

Your book is already written. Turning it into an audiobook opens a new revenue stream with minimal additional effort from you.

Reach New Audiences

Millions of people consume content exclusively as audio — commuters, exercisers, visually impaired readers. An audiobook edition puts your work in front of a completely different audience segment.

02

Increase Revenue Per Title

Audiobooks typically sell at 2-3x the price of ebooks. With AI production costs a fraction of traditional studio costs, the margin opportunity is substantial — especially for backlist titles.

03

Faster Time to Market

Traditional audiobook production takes months. Our AI-powered pipeline can have your book in audio production within days of receiving the manuscript, and delivered weeks ahead of traditional timelines.

04

Platform-Ready Delivery

We produce files that meet every major platform's technical specifications — Audible, Apple Books, Spotify, Findaway Voices. No back-and-forth with platform QC teams.
